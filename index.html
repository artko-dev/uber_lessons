8 reasons to start working with us

No cash, payment is automatic

The cost of the trip is paid by the passenger from a linked bank card, i.e. there are no risks associated with the transportation of cash for both the passenger and the driver; you do not need to look for small change for change.

Commission 8% + BONUSES

We have a minimum commission and pay bonuses for each trip; some of our competitors keep bonuses for themselves. We work honestly and with us drivers earn more!

High income with guaranteed payments

We pay wages to the driver’s personal bank card! You do not need to register an LLC, individual entrepreneur, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and maintain a bank account.

Work at a convenient time, no schedules

You simply press the exit line button to start working, when you decide to finish, press exit the line. You can work part-time Use only in the morning, evening, weekends or holidays!

Comfortable, transparent and simple connection

We connect remotely and free of charge, you do not need to come to us! You send us copies of documents, we register you and send you connection information, in 1-2 days you can start working!

Always a large number of orders

The application shows where there are currently the most orders and where increased rates apply. You will not have downtime and idle runs. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflict people who are a pleasure to carry.

24/7 support

Uber offers 24/7 driver support via email. If something is unclear, you can always ask a question through an On-line consultation.

Without checkers and stickers

Without checkers and stickers You don’t need to change the color, install “checkers” on the car and cover it with stickers.
